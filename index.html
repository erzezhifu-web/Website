New federal legislation may change how Americans register to vote. This free tool walks you through every step — from checking your documents to submitting your registration.

📋 SAVE America Act Update (Feb 11, 2026): The House passed the Safeguard American Voter Eligibility Act 218–213. If the Senate passes it and it's signed into law, registering to vote will require in-person documentary proof of citizenship — a passport or birth certificate — for every registration or update. Over 21 million eligible voters don't have these documents readily available. Get prepared now, while it's still easy.
